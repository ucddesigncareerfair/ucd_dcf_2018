HISTORY

Design Career Club is thrilled to host the 4th annual Design Career Fair at UC Davis Art Annex. Though the event is relatively new each has gone through considerable change which has allow us to experience one memorial fair after another. This is a look back at all the incredible people who made these experience possible. We hope to carry on the tradition and create more opportunity for our up-and-coming students to reach bold heights.

In 2016, after two years of organizing successful student-run career fairs for our Design Department, the UC Davis Design Career Club was founded by our first director, Tiffany Tieu.
